Title: Clinical Guidelines for Obesity Management
Source: International Obesity Treatment Council
Author: IOTC Guidelines Committee
Date: 2024-02-15
Type: Clinical Guideline
OBESITY CLASSIFICATION AND MANAGEMENT
BMI Classification:

Overweight: 25.0-29.9 kg/m²
Class I Obesity: 30.0-34.9 kg/m²
Class II Obesity: 35.0-39.9 kg/m²
Class III Obesity: ≥40 kg/m²

Assessment Protocol:

Initial Evaluation

Detailed weight history
Eating patterns
Physical activity level
Comorbidity screening
Psychological assessment


Treatment Algorithm

Lifestyle intervention
Dietary modification
Physical activity prescription
Behavioral therapy
Pharmacotherapy consideration
Bariatric surgery evaluation



Monitoring Requirements:

Monthly weight measurements
Body composition analysis
Metabolic parameter tracking
Comorbidity assessment
Quality of life evaluation
