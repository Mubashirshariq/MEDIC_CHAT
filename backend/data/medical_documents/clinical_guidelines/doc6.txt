Title: Clinical Practice Guidelines for Type 2 Diabetes Management
Source: World Health Organization Guidelines Repository
Author: WHO Diabetes Expert Committee
Date: 2024-01-15
Type: Clinical Guideline
Comprehensive Guidelines for Type 2 Diabetes Management
DIAGNOSTIC CRITERIA

Primary Diagnostic Tests

Fasting Plasma Glucose (FPG) ≥ 126 mg/dL (7.0 mmol/L)
2-hour Plasma Glucose ≥ 200 mg/dL (11.1 mmol/L) during OGTT
HbA1c ≥ 6.5% (48 mmol/mol)
Random Plasma Glucose ≥ 200 mg/dL with symptoms


Risk Factors Assessment

Family history of diabetes
Obesity (BMI ≥ 30 kg/m²)
Physical inactivity
Age ≥ 45 years
Hypertension (≥140/90 mmHg)
Dyslipidemia



TREATMENT PROTOCOL

Initial Management

Lifestyle modifications
Metformin (if not contraindicated)
Blood glucose monitoring
HbA1c target < 7%


Treatment Escalation
First-line: Metformin
Second-line options:

Sulfonylureas
DPP-4 inhibitors
SGLT2 inhibitors
GLP-1 receptor agonists


Monitoring Schedule

HbA1c every 3-6 months
Annual comprehensive foot examination
Annual eye examination
Regular cardiovascular assessment
