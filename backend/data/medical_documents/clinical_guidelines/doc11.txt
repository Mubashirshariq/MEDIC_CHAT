Title: Chronic Kidney Disease Management Guidelines
Source: International Nephrology Association
Author: INA Guidelines Committee
Date: 2024-02-20
Type: Clinical Guideline
CKD STAGING AND MANAGEMENT
Disease Staging:
Stage 1: eGFR ≥90 mL/min/1.73m²
Stage 2: eGFR 60-89 mL/min/1.73m²
Stage 3a: eGFR 45-59 mL/min/1.73m²
Stage 3b: eGFR 30-44 mL/min/1.73m²
Stage 4: eGFR 15-29 mL/min/1.73m²
Stage 5: eGFR <15 mL/min/1.73m²
Management Protocol:

Risk Factor Control

Blood pressure management
Glycemic control
Lipid management
Lifestyle modification


Complications Management

Anemia
Bone mineral disorders
Cardiovascular disease
Malnutrition
