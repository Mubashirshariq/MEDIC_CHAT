HEART DISEASE: WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW
Understanding Heart Disease
Heart disease includes various conditions affecting your heart and blood vessels. The most common type is coronary artery disease, which can lead to heart attack.
Key Information:

Warning Signs

Chest pain or discomfort
Shortness of breath
Upper body discomfort
Nausea, lightheadedness
Cold sweats


Risk Factors
Controllable:

High blood pressure
High cholesterol
Smoking
Obesity
Physical inactivity

Uncontrollable:

Age
Gender
Family history


Prevention Strategies

Regular exercise
Heart-healthy diet
Stress management
Regular check-ups
Medication adherence
