PREVENTIVE HEALTHCARE GUIDELINES
Understanding Preventive Care
Preventive care focuses on maintaining health and catching potential problems early, when they're most treatable.
Key Components:

Regular Screenings

Blood pressure checks
Cholesterol testing
Cancer screenings
Diabetes testing
Mental health assessments


Immunizations
Adult Vaccines:

Annual flu shot
Tdap/Td booster
Shingles vaccine
Pneumonia vaccine
COVID-19 vaccination


Lifestyle Practices

Regular exercise
Healthy diet
Stress management
Sleep hygiene
Tobacco avoidance
