LIVING WITH TYPE 2 DIABETES
What is Type 2 Diabetes?
Type 2 diabetes occurs when your body cannot properly use insulin or doesn't make enough insulin. This causes glucose to build up in your blood instead of being used for energy.
Common Symptoms:

Early Signs

Increased thirst and urination
Unusual hunger
Unexplained weight loss
Fatigue
Blurred vision
